About

I am a data scientist. My passion is data analytics, especially related to mental health. I use machine learning to help detect, assess, and treat several psychiatric illnesses.

My current focus has been centered on using machine learning (ML) applications to better understand the underlying conditions of psychiatric disorders. Specifically, I have used computational modeling to identify early biological or cognitive indicators that may manifest during the early stages of a given illness. As a data scientist in Psychiatry, I have worked with various modes of data, including functional neuroimaging, speech audio, text, and cognitive assessments. As a result, I have experience with natural language processing, time series analysis, computer vision and signal processing. I bring a wide variety of tools to the table, and I am creative in my approaches. I hope to use my skillset in part with a team of passionate data scientists at a company whose focus is on making the world a better place.
